Workshops
Our workshops are designed to equip faculty members and students with relevant competencies to use SUTD's Educational Technology resources to enrich teaching and learning.



eDimension
Academic Media Studio



eDimension is SUTD's Learning Management System (LMS), which is based on Blackboard. Our workshops cover features, tools and best practices when using eDimension. 

Each workshop includes hands-on activities designed to take you from a blank course to a finished mock-up course using sample content and learning activities.

To make the most of our workshops, faculty members are advised to bring their laptops.
