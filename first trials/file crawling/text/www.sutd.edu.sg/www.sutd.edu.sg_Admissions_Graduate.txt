Our graduate programmes move beyond traditional education boundaries and emphasise a multi-disciplinary focus - design, innovation and entrepreneurship are seamlessly integrated into the curriculum.

                    Master Programmes
                













                                    Research the next generation of computing, photonics, biosensing, manufacturing, and AI technologies by learning how to design at the nanoscale.
Dual Masters in Nano-Electronics and Design








                                    Investigate deeply into technology and research infused design, through practice, studio and thesis elements, to realize design futures.  
Master of Architecture








                                    Offers both fresh graduates and working professionals the opportunity to explore independent, research-oriented solutions to existing engineering challenges.
Master of Engineering (Research)








                                    A unique graduate program that aims to promote design thinking and innovation across several fields of knowledge.
Master of Innovation by Design








                                    Conduct cyber security research and training at SUTD’s world-class testbeds to learn how to design, analyse and manage cyber security for critical networks and infrastructure.
Master of Science in Security by Design








                                    Be part of the next generation of urban researchers, analysts and practitioners who will plan and shape the cities of the future.
Master of Science in Urban Science, Policy and Planning








                                    Develop design innovation expertise intertwined with IT security, fostering skills relevancy in the cybersecurity industry where threats and technologies evolve rapidly.
Master of Science in Tech & Design (Cybersecurity)








                                    Upgrade your skillset with knowledge in sustainable product design and management to fast-track your careers or entrepreneurial pursuits as technically-grounded leaders and innovators.
Master of Science in Tech & Design (Sustainable Product Design)










                    PhD Programmes
                













                                    Collaborate with the best minds in a fluid and stimulating setting and conduct breakthrough research that will make a difference to the world.
SUTD PhD Programme








                                    Experience the best of both world-class institutions - SUTD’s innovative and flexible curriculum and NUS’s established graduate programme and research track record.
SUTD-NUS Joint PhD Programme








                                    Provides graduate students with industry-relevant training, which is important to translate research & development (R&D) into tangible products, systems and services.
SUTD Engineering Doctorate (EngD) Programme










                INDUSTRIAL PROGRAMMES
            











                                    Be a full-time salaried employee at partnering companies in Singapore while pursuing full-time postgraduate studies at SUTD.
Economic Development Board (EDB) Industrial Postgraduate Programme










                PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
            











                                    The suite of Professional Development Courses provide breadth beyond engineering and technology modules. 
Professional Development Short Courses
