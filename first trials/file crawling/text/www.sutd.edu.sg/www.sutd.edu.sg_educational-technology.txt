Welcome to SUTD's Educational Technology Microsite
The Educational Technology Team supports faculty members and students in blended learning and classroom technologies such as eDimension (Learning Management System), Online Exams, Plagiarism Tool, vDimension, Learning Catalytics, Subject and Instructor Evaluation and the Academic Media Studio.
In addition, the Educational Technology team also offers workshops and support for faculty members and staff.
The Educational Technology team is part of Undergraduate Studies, which serves under the guidance and leadership of Associate Provost (Education) Professor Pey Kin Leong.

Educational Technology Support

Email: edi_admin@sutd.edu.sg
Operating hours: 8.30 AM to 5.30 PM (Mon to Fri, excluding Public Holidays)
You can find more information about our Learning Solutions on our microsite.
