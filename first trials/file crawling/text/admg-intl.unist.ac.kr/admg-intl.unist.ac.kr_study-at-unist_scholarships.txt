Scholarship






UNIST Scholarship
Selection criteria

Graduate students who are admitted as Government scholarship student or UNIST scholarship student.
Graduate students who are enrolled within 4 semesters for Master’s Program, 8 semesters for Doctoral Program, 12 semesters for MS-Ph.D Program.
Students who received the academic warning or achieved fewer than 3 credits in the previous semester shall be excluded.
Students who have been placed under probation or stronger shall be excluded.

Details
Scholarship funded by Korean government

Tuition fee waiver(KRW 7,680,000/year)
Stipend offer
Master: More than KRW 9,600,000/year
Doctor: More than KRW 13,200,000/year
※ The stipend will be provided monthly.
※ The above is based on the minimum amount and the additional amount can be provided differently according to the participating Lab
※ Professional·Special graduate school, Management Engineering students, Students who are enrolled exceeding the class period are not included
Students pursuing Doctoral degree are eligible for technical research personnel recruitment
Dormitory on campus is available for graduate students

Scholarship funded by UNIST

Tuition fee support(KRW 7,680,000/year)
※ Successful applicants pay the tuition at their own expense. And when Stipend is paid during the semester, their advisors support the divided amount corresponding to the tuition.
Stipend offer
Master: More than KRW 17,280,000/year
Doctor: More than KRW 20,880,000/year
※ The stipend will be provided monthly.
※ The above is based on the minimum amount and the additional amount can be provided differently according to the participating Lab
※ Professional·Special graduate school, Management Engineering students, Students who are enrolled exceeding the class period are not included
Students pursuing Doctoral degree are eligible for technical research personnel recruitment
Dormitory on campus is available for graduate students

Scholarship funded by Others(Company, Institute, Yourself, etc.)

Tuition fee shall be remitted.(KRW 7,680,000/year)
Non-payment of Stipend
Non-incorporated in Technical Research Personnel
Dormitory on campus is available for graduate students

※ Scholarships are subject to change and will be given under UNIST regulations.
※ Students who benefits other scholarship shall be excluded according to the regulations.
※ Please contact us for the detailed scholarship information.
TA Scholarship
Selection criteria

Selected by the standard of Teaching Assistant operating plan

Scholarship Amount


Head TA: Master’s 300,000 KRW per month, Doctoral 500,000 KRW per month
 R TA: Master’s 200,000 KRW per month, Doctoral 300,000 KRW per month
TA : Master’s 150,000 KRW per month, Doctoral 250,000 KRW per month


※ The amount above is approximate and will change according to the plan
