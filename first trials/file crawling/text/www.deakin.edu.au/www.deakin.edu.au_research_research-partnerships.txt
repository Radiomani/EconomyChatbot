Achieve your business goals with a Deakin partnership



Whatever the size of your organisation, we can help you grow your ideas from concept to reality. Tap into the best and brightest minds from our faculties, institutes and research centres. Access industrial scale facilities and co-location opportunities, as well as our world-class, impact-driven research.In partnering with Deakin, you’ll gain a collaborator who is open and flexible to your needs, understands your goals, and shares your ambition.With proven success in tailoring practical interventions and launching scalable commercial applications, we’re consistently delivering transformational solutions to local and global challenges.


Our PartnershipsAs Victoria’s first regional university, we’ve built strong partnerships with businesses, government, and communities not only across the world but in the Geelong and Warrnambool regions. From Barwon Health to local carbon fibre and hydrogen industries, we’re committed to supporting businesses, creating jobs and driving economic and social development in regional Victoria. Restless for local success, we strive to enable our local industries and communities to unleash their ideas and innovations for global impact.

Discover how we can work together



No matter your question, challenge or vision, our research community at Deakin is ready to work closely with you to deliver impact.  We’re out in communities helping to drive positive change. We’re in laboratories and on factory floors testing prototypes for scale up. Join thousands of companies – across a diverse range of industries – who have benefited from our ingenuity and multidisciplinary research.


Research for industry and governmentTogether we can better understand and address the needs of the communities we serve. Connecting our people and capabilities to your ideas, we develop practical and effective solutions for the major challenges facing communities in Australia and abroad.With over 500 active partnerships across 57 countries, join our rapidly expanding strategic partnerships network and leverage our capabilities, connections, and facilities.

Access leading research facilities



Your partnership with Deakin opens the door to multimillion dollar research facilities and infrastructure, some of which can’t be found anywhere outside of our campuses.Our facilities are shared by researchers from each of our four faculties, giving you access to future-focused technology and new ways of thinking about your question or product. Some of our unique facilities include:Carbon Nexus – a world-first, open-access facility with an unparalleled suite of carbon fibre research technologyBatTRI-Hub – an innovative research centre focused on advanced battery prototyping and commercialisationCognitive neuroscience facilities – featuring EEG, EMG, TMS, near infrared spectroscopy and much moreMotion.Lab – research, art and movement-based technology intersect at this reality-bending VR facility
