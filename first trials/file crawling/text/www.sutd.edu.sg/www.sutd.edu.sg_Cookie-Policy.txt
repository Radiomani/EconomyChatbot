What is a cookie?
A cookie is a small packet of information that’s stored on your device when you visit a website. 
 
Why do we use cookies?
We use cookies to understand how you consume contents on our website. This helps us to understand your surfing habits and to improve the website’s usability in order to promote the University more efficiently and to optimise your browsing experience.  
 



COOKIE TYPE
DESCRIPTION


Strictly necessary cookies
Deliver information and services securely and optimally


Functionality and Performance cookies
Improve the usability and performance of website


Advertising and Remarketing cookies
Tracking pixels that set cookies to assist with delivering relevant online advertising based on your consumption.




Singapore University of Technology and Design does not share any information to third party and personal data is not identifiable from these cookies. You may opt out of all advertising cookies by modifying your browser settings.
