Pool and/or Fitness Centre Memberships 


To use the pool or fitness centre, staff and their immediate family members, as well as visitors on official business at SUTD, may sign up for annual, half-yearly or monthly pool and/or fitness centre memberships.
For more information:

SUTD staff/students - refer to SUTD central 
Visitors - email to sports_booking@sutd.edu.sg


Booking of Facilities
Only students and staff of the University may book the facilities for recreational use. These bookings are non-transferable. 

Each user is entitled to book up to two hours per calendar week per facility. Bookings can be made up to 14 days in advance, with a minimum notice of three working days.

For SUTD staff and students, please book via IBMS. For all other users and booking related enquires, please email to sports_booking@sutd.edu.sg.

For information on facility booking charges, please visit https://tinyurl.com/src-sutdstudent (students) or https://tinyurl.com/src-sutdstaff2 (staff).

The facilities (except for the fitness centre and pool) will continue to be open for free play (in as is condition) by all staff and students during operating hours when there are no bookings.

SUTD Student Groups who wish to book facilities for competitions and/or training are to approach their respective officers from the Office of Student Life. 

Rules & Regulations
Users are to note and obey the rules and regulations of the respective facilities and/or instructions by authorised staff. 

Users are to be properly attired for their respective activities. No food and drinks (except for bottled drinks) are allowed inside the sports and recreational facilities. Users are strongly encouraged to use the lockers that are available throughout the recreation centre and not to leave their valuables unattended. 

Please report any damage to the Campus Facilities Team (help-facilities@sutd.edu.sg) or 6303 6699 (during office hours) immediately. 

The University reserves the right to cancel any booking without any notice. 

Lightning
Please stop all outdoor activity and move indoors immediately when the lightning warning blinking lights and/or siren are activated. You may resume activity when the warning lights go off.

For your safety, you are advised not to use outdoor facilities in the event of wet weather.
