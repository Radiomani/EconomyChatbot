The ‘Student ID Number’ inquiry of freshman for 2022 Graduate Fall semester(Available after 10:00am(KST) on Friday, July 29th) 557Views


7월 28, 2022






The ‘Student ID Number’ inquiry of new graduate students in the Fall semester of 2022 is available after 10:00am(KST) on Friday, July 29th.
Please refer to the student number inquiry procedure below.
– Access to the site for student number inquiry: [Go to website]
* Just look up your student number and don’t click or touch anything else!
* Freshman orientation materials will be sent by e-mail to new students from the Education Affairs Team during 7.29.(Fri) or 8.1.(Mon).
